Fishing

Fishing is a popular recreational activity that involves catching fish from bodies of water such as rivers, lakes, and oceans. It can be done for sport, food, or relaxation. There are various techniques and equipment used in fishing, including rods, reels, bait, and lures. Some common types of fishing include fly fishing, ice fishing, and deep-sea fishing. Fishing can also have environmental impacts, so it's important to practice sustainable fishing methods to preserve aquatic ecosystems.
